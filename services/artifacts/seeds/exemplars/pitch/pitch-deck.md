# Pitch Deck — Northwind Cloud (GOLD EXEMPLAR)

Reference for the structure, depth, and tone of an excellent **pitch deck**. Fictional
company — do NOT copy this content. Note the craft: open with the problem (not the
product), every slide title is an **assertion**, one idea per slide, and the persuasive
arc ends in a specific, time-bound call to action. Presenter detail lives in the
**speaker notes**.

---

## Slide 1 — Cover (statement layout)
**Title:** Your data stays yours. Your AI ships anyway.
**Subtitle:** Northwind Cloud — sovereign AI infrastructure
**Notes (presenter):** Open on the tension the buyer already feels. Say the company name once, then move straight to their problem.

## Slide 2 — Problem / why-now
**Title:** Regulated teams are forced to choose between AI speed and data control
- New AI mandates collide with data-residency rules
- Cloud AI means data leaves the perimeter — a non-starter for regulated buyers
- The gap is widening every quarter
**Notes:** Make the buyer nod three times before you mention your product. Why-now = the regulatory deadline.

## Slide 3 — Solution
**Title:** Northwind runs enterprise AI inside your own environment
- Models deployed in your VPC or region
- Your data never leaves your boundary
- Full capability, zero egress
**Notes:** One sentence of what it is. Resist listing features here — that's slide 4.

## Slide 4 — How it works
**Title:** One integration, three steps, complete control
- Connect your environment (1 day)
- Deploy the model catalog (managed)
- Call one API — we handle scaling and updates
**Notes:** Show it's simple. A three-step mental model beats an architecture diagram in a pitch.

## Slide 5 — Proof
**Title:** Already running production AI for three regulated enterprises
- A top-5 bank: 40M inferences/mo, zero data egress
- A health network: HIPAA-cleared in 6 weeks
- 99.98% uptime across all deployments
**Notes:** Lead with the most regulated logo you have. Proof beats promises for this buyer.

## Slide 6 — Differentiation
**Title:** The only option with self-hosting control and zero operational burden
- vs. cloud AI: your data stays in your boundary
- vs. DIY: no MLOps team required
- The sovereignty wall is the moat
**Notes:** Frame against the two real alternatives the buyer is weighing, not a feature grid.

## Slide 7 — Pricing
**Title:** Predictable platform pricing that scales with usage, not headcount
- Flat platform fee + usage-based inference
- No per-seat tax
- Pilot pricing available for the first 90 days
**Notes:** Keep it to the shape of pricing; exact numbers go in the follow-up, not the room.

## Slide 8 — The ask / CTA
**Title:** Let's run a 4-week production pilot in your environment — risk-free
- Scope: one use case, your data, your region
- Success criteria agreed up front
- Start date: within two weeks of today
**Notes:** Close with a concrete, low-risk next step and a date. Ask for the pilot, not "thoughts."
