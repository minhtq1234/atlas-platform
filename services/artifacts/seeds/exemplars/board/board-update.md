# Board Update — Q3 FY26 · Northwind Cloud (GOLD EXEMPLAR)

Reference for the structure, depth, and tone of an excellent **board deck**. Fictional
company — do NOT copy this content. Note the craft: every content-slide title is an
**assertion** (the takeaway), each slide holds one idea, and the presenter detail lives
in the **speaker notes**, not on the slide.

---

## Slide 1 — Cover
**Title:** Northwind Cloud — Q3 FY26 Board Update
**Subtitle:** October 2026 · Confidential
**Notes (presenter):** Two decisions needed today — EU launch timing and the Series B raise. Frame those up front so the discussion lands where it matters.

## Slide 2 — TL;DR
**Title:** Q3 beat plan on revenue and retention; one call needed on EU timing
- Revenue $12.4M, +6% vs plan
- Net revenue retention 118%
- One ask: approve a one-quarter EU launch slip to de-risk certification
**Notes:** Deliver the three headlines in 30 seconds before any detail. If the board only remembers one slide, it should be this one.

## Slide 3 — KPIs vs. plan
**Title:** Every headline metric landed at or above plan except EU pipeline
- Revenue $12.4M (plan $11.7M) · +6%
- Gross margin 74% (plan 72%)
- NRR 118% (plan 112%)
- EU pipeline $3.1M (plan $5.0M) — the one miss
**Notes:** Anchor each number to plan; the board reads variance, not absolutes. Flag the EU miss here so slide 6 isn't a surprise.

## Slide 4 — Progress by workstream
**Title:** Three of four workstreams are green; EU is the exception
- Product: shipped usage-based billing, 2 weeks early
- GTM: enterprise logos +9 (Acme, Globex, Initech)
- Reliability: 99.98% uptime, zero Sev-1s
- EU: certification delayed (see next)
**Notes:** Keep this to status + one proof point each. Detail belongs in the appendix.

## Slide 5 — Risk
**Title:** EU certification slipped a quarter; mitigation is funded and underway
- Root cause: auditor backlog, not a product gap
- Mitigation: parallel audit track + interim single-region offer
- Exposure: ~$1.8M pipeline pushed to Q1, not lost
**Notes:** Be direct about the miss and lead with the mitigation. Boards forgive slips they see being managed.

## Slide 6 — The ask
**Title:** We request board approval to slip EU launch to Q1 and open the Series B
- Approve EU launch: Q4 → Q1 (de-risks certification)
- Authorize Series B raise: target $15M
- Decision needed today to hold the Q1 timeline
**Notes:** State the ask as a clear yes/no. Have the term-sheet summary ready in the appendix if asked.

## Slide 7 — Next steps
**Title:** With approval, we close the raise by December and launch EU in Q1
- Series B: term sheet by Nov, close by Dec (Owner: CEO)
- EU: parallel audit complete Jan (Owner: VP Eng)
- Board check-in: December
**Notes:** End on owners + dates. Every next step has a name against it.
